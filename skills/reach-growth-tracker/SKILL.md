## Purpose
Systematically measure and optimize growth across all acquisition channels. Build a dashboard mindset — not one-off campaigns, but continuous measurement and iteration. Apply the Prediction pillar (hypothesis → measure → change) to reach efforts. Double down on what works, cut what doesn't.

---

## Core Frameworks

### Hunters vs Fishermen — Measurement Mindset
Systems beat individual hunters. Build measurement systems that reveal which channels work, not gut feel. Track everything. Compare. Optimize.

### OBE Metrics (Own, Buy, Earn)
Separate traffic performance by source:
- **Own:** Email, SMS, community, organic referrals (highest quality, highest LTV)
- **Buy:** Paid ads (Google, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn), sponsored content (fastest scale, requires budget)
- **Earn:** Partnerships, affiliates, JVs, organic word-of-mouth, PR (free, slowest ramp, highest trust)

Healthy mix = resilience. Imbalance reveals risks.

### CAC by Channel
Know your Customer Acquisition Cost for each channel.
```
CAC = (Ad Spend + Labor) / New Customers

Then compare:
- CAC vs LTV (Should be LTV > 3x CAC)
- CAC by channel (Scale best channels first)
- CAC payback period (Recoup cost in <6 months)
```

### Traffic Funnel Scorecard (Matt Grey)
Track the full funnel per channel:
- Clicks / Impressions = CTR
- Leads / Clicks = Lead Conv Rate
- Customers / Leads = Sales Conv Rate
- Revenue / Customers = AOV

Find breakage points per channel.

### 12-Week Launch (Noah Kagan)
Concentrate a growth push into 12 weeks:
- Week 1-2: Pre-launch (audience building, list warmup)
- Week 3-8: Main push (content, ads, partnerships, daily metrics review)
- Week 9-12: Close-out (last-minute offers, final conversions, data analysis)

Measure milestone by milestone. Velocity matters more than perfection.

### Hypothesis Testing on Acquisition
Apply Prediction pillar to reach:
- **Hypothesis:** "Expanding our Facebook audience to age 35-45 will reduce CAC by 20%"
- **Measure:** Run cohort test for 2 weeks
- **Change:** Scale or kill based on data

## Workflows

### 1. Growth Dashboard (Weekly)
Build a live dashboard showing:
- **Traffic:** Visits, sessions, unique users (by channel)
- **Lead Gen:** Leads captured, lead quality score, email unsubscribe rate
- **Conversions:** Paying customers, trial-to-paid rate, repeat purchase rate
- **CAC:** Cost per channel, blended CAC, CAC trend month-over-month
- **LTV Metrics:** Average customer lifetime value, repeat purchase rate, churn rate
- **Efficiency:** LTV/CAC ratio (Target: >3x), payback period (Target: <6 months)

Refresh every Friday. Identify trends. Spot underperformers.

Output: Google Sheets dashboard + email summary of top insights.

### 2. Channel Scorecard
Rate each channel on a consistent rubric:
- **Volume:** How much traffic does this generate? (Trending up/flat/down?)
- **Quality:** What % convert to leads? What % to customers? (Best to worst channels?)
- **CAC:** Cost per customer (Cheapest to most expensive?)
- **Scalability:** Can we 10x spend and maintain ROI? (Yes/no/maybe?)
- **Sustainability:** Does it depend on a third-party algorithm or our own owned list?

Quarterly refresh. Reallocate budget based on scorecard.

Output: Channel scorecard with investment recommendations.

### 3. 12-Week Sprint Design
Plan a concentrated growth push:
- **Weeks 1-2:** Audience prep (warm list, presell content, partnership outreach)
- **Weeks 3-8:** Full-throttle push (ads running, daily metrics, 2-3 tests per week)
- **Weeks 9-12:** Close-out (final offers, scarcity angle, last emails, deep data dive)

Milestones:
- End of Week 2: Pre-launch audience size, partnership confirmations
- End of Week 8: Total leads generated, CAC by channel, best-performing creative
- End of Week 12: Total customers, revenue, LTV early signals, plan next sprint

Output: 12-week sprint plan + weekly progress tracker.

### 4. OBE Balance Check
Is your traffic mix healthy?
- Own (email, SMS, community): Target 40-50% of traffic (highest LTV)
- Buy (paid ads): Target 30-40% of traffic (fastest scale)
- Earn (partnerships, affiliates, organic): Target 10-20% of traffic (free moat)

If Own <30%, you're too dependent on paid. If Buy >60%, margins are at risk.

Output: OBE traffic mix scorecard + rebalancing recommendations.

### 5. Growth Experiment Log
Track acquisition experiments using hypothesis methodology:
- **Experiment:** Name (e.g., "Facebook audience expansion to age 35-45")
- **Hypothesis:** Expected impact ("20% CAC reduction")
- **Duration:** Test period (2 weeks)
- **Metric:** Primary KPI (CAC for that cohort)
- **Result:** What happened? (CAC down 18%, statistical significance?)
- **Decision:** Scale, kill, or iterate?
- **Learning:** One sentence takeaway

Review bi-weekly. Compound learnings.

Output: Experiment log in Google Sheets + monthly learning summary email.
