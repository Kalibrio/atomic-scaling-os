## Purpose
Build systematic funnels, traffic acquisition systems, and strategic partnerships that scale revenue without hiring. Focus on systems (fishermen mindset) over one-off campaigns (hunters). Implement Peak Sales Machine framework with automated follow-up sequences at every stage.

---

## Core Frameworks

### Hunters vs Fishermen
**Build systems, don't chase leads.** Systems scale; individual hunting doesn't. Design repeatable, measurable funnel architecture that compounds over time.

### Peak Sales Machine Funnel (Russell Brunson + Danielle Leslie)
```
Lead Magnet → Squeeze Page (83% conversion)
  → Sales Page + Order Bump (16% conv, 25% bump take rate)
  → One-Time Offer (20% take rate)
  → Follow-up Funnel at EACH stage
```
Each stage has its own follow-up sequence. Most sales happen in follow-up, not first touch.

### OBE Traffic Model
- **Own:** Email list, SMS, community (highest control, highest LTV)
- **Buy:** Paid ads, sponsorships (quickest scale, requires capital)
- **Earn:** Partnerships, affiliates, JVs, organic (slowest, free, most sustainable)

**Strategy:** Start with Earn (build relationships), fund Buy (with revenue), build Own (long-term moat).

### Dream 100
Identify 100 dream partners/influencers who already have your target audience. Systematically build relationships. Reverse-engineer their traffic into partnerships. (Chet Holmes + Russell Brunson concept.)

### Follow-up Funnel Magic
- 80% of ad clicks happen in first 3 seconds (creative matters most)
- Most conversions happen in follow-up, not first touch
- Automate follow-up at every stage: Lead Magnet → Squeeze → Sales → OTO → Evergreen
- Track conversion rates and iterate using Prediction pillar methodology

### Anchoring
Make a series of smaller offers before the real offer to anchor value perception and increase conversion rate significantly.

## Workflows

### 1. Peak Sales Machine Design
Build the full funnel step by step:
- Lead Magnet: What are you giving away? (Goal: relevance + email capture)
- Squeeze Page: Single value prop + email field (Target: 83% conversion)
- Sales Page + Order Bump: Main offer + small bump (Target: 16% conversion, 25% bump take rate)
- One-Time Offer: Final upsell (Target: 20% take rate)
- Follow-up sequences: Email/SMS automations after each decision point

Output: Funnel map with conversion targets, copy frameworks, and follow-up sequence outlines.

### 2. Dream 100 List Builder
- Identify 100 dream partners (influencers, content creators, strategic partners with your audience)
- Categorize by tier (Platinum, Gold, Silver)
- Plan outreach strategy per tier
- Track relationship status and partnership outcomes
- Automate tracking in Google Sheets for ongoing management

Output: Dream 100 sheet with contact info, social metrics, outreach status, and partnership ideas.

### 3. OBE Traffic Audit
Score your current traffic mix:
- Own (email, SMS, community): % of total traffic, CAC, LTV
- Buy (paid ads, sponsorships): % of total, ROI, scalability
- Earn (partnerships, organic, affiliates): % of total, velocity, time-to-revenue

Identify imbalances. (Over-dependent on Buy = risky. Not enough Own = no moat.)

Output: Traffic health scorecard with recommendations.

### 4. Follow-up Sequence Builder
Design automated sequences for:
- Post-lead-magnet: 3-day welcome sequence (build trust, ascend to product)
- Post-squeeze: 5-email sales sequence (objection handling, proof, scarcity)
- Post-purchase: 7-step onboarding + upsell sequence (maximize LTV, reduce churn)
- Post-OTO-decline: 3-email re-offer sequence (different angle, urgency)

Output: Sequence templates with subject lines, copy angles, timing, and metrics.

### 5. Funnel Scorecard
Track conversion at every step vs benchmarks:
- Lead Magnet → Squeeze (Target: 83%)
- Squeeze → Sales Page (Target: 80%+)
- Sales Page → Purchase (Target: 16%)
- Order Bump take rate (Target: 25%)
- OTO take rate (Target: 20%)

Monthly review. Identify bottlenecks. Hypothesis test improvements.
